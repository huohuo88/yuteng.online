About Yuteng Monster Survivors

Yuteng Monster Survivors is an immersive online gaming experience that puts your survival skills to the test. In this action-packed adventure, you'll face endless waves of monsters in a constantly evolving world. With each wave becoming progressively challenging, you'll need to collect power-ups, upgrade your character, and employ strategic gameplay to survive as long as possible.

Developed using cutting-edge Unity technology, Yuteng Monster Survivors offers smooth gameplay, stunning visuals, and an engaging progression system that will keep you coming back for more. The game features a variety of unique characters, each with special abilities and playstyles to master.

How to Play

Getting started with Yuteng Monster Survivors is simple:

1.

Controls: Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character (on desktop). On mobile, use the virtual joystick that appears on the screen.

2.

Combat: Your character attacks automatically. Position yourself strategically to maximize damage while avoiding enemy attacks.

3.

Level Up: Collect experience gems dropped by defeated enemies to level up. Each level, you'll get to choose between three random upgrades.

4.

Power-ups: Special items occasionally appear on the battlefield. Collect them for temporary or permanent boosts.

5.

Survive: The goal is to survive as long as possible while defeating as many monsters as you can. Your score increases with each enemy defeated.
